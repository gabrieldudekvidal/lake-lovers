4 Days Mardi Himal Trek

Day 1 - Phedi to Forest Camp

Hike for 11 km through forest and local villages. Immerse yourself in local Nepali culture along the way.

Day 2 - Forest Camp to High Camp

Hike for 9 km. Explore dense forest and wildlife. If the day is clear there are high changes of seeing the mountains algong the way.

Day 3 - High Camp to Upper View Point

Hike for 2.5 km. Start early in the morning for a breathtaking hike to the Upper View Point. Enjoy panoramic views of Mardi Himal, Machapuchare, and the Annapurna range.

Day 4 - High Camp to Sidhing

Hike for 11km. Descend through beautiful forests and quiet trails to the peaceful village of Sidhing. There, we can share a Jeep and return to Pokhara.
